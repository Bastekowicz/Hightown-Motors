The Car Specialists

We stock quality new and used cars. The majority of our used cars come with a 12 month warranty. Our stock includes most makes and models of cars.

Hightown Motors is a small family-run car business. It opened in 1998 on the Hightown Road in Newtownabbey and employs 13 people. Hightown Motors stock a range of cars, including classic and high end cars, as well as family cars and hatchbacks. They offer a range of cars to cater for every budget.
